Strategically located near Biju Patnaik International Airport, Hotel Pentagon is one of the most preferred budget hotels in Bhubaneswar. Leisure travellers and pilgrims opt for this hotel owing to its:

  • Clean & hygienic rooms, disinfected at regular intervals
  • Prime location
  • Conference facilities
  • In-house restaurant

Where we are Located
  • Hotel Pentagon is located at Kalpana Square, Laxmisagar, Bhubaneswar
  • Biju Patnaik International Airport and Bhubaneswar railway station are both within a radius of 4 km of the hotel
  • Some of the nearby landmarks are Syndicate Bank (300 m), The Revenue Inspector Office (400 m), Allahabad Bank (550 m) and Mahadev Temple (600 m)
Our Rooms and Amenities
  • Hotel Pentagon offers hygienic rooms that are well-lit and come with a host of modern amenities
  • Air conditioner, flat-screen TV with a satellite connection, wardrobe and attached washroom with complimentary toiletries are common to all rooms
  • The hotel also has ample parking space, a banquet hall and a reliable power backup for guests
Where to Eat
  • An in-house restaurant is available for guests staying at Hotel Pentagon
  • Guests can also suffice their untimely hunger in the comfort of their rooms by ordering food from the food menu which is provided in every room
  • Nearby restaurants are Hotel Bansidhar (350 m), Truptee Veg Restaurant (500 m), Taste of China (1 km) and Narula's Restaurant (1.6 km)
What You'll Love
  • Sightseeing: Odisha State Museum (700 m), Lingaraj Temple (3.2 km) and Nandankanan Zoological Park (17.7 km) are some of the major attractions in Bhubaneswar
  • Shopping: Bapuji Nagar (1.5 km), Unit 2 Market Building (2.2 km), Ekamra Haat (3.5 km) and Shahid Nagar Market (3.6 km) are a few of the most popular shopping destinations near the hotel
